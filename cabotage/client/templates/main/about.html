About Cabotage

Cabotage is a self-hosted Platform as a Service (PaaS) that lets you deploy and manage applications on your own infrastructure with the ease of a managed platform.

Built on top of Kubernetes, Cabotage handles container builds, deployments, scaling, environment configuration, and more -- giving you full control without the complexity.
